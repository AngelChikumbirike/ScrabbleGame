Implementing a Bit of Scrabble with Drag-and-Drop

How to Play:

  • Create one word with no gaps between the letters. ✅
  • The word must contain at least 2 letters. ✅
  • The word must be found in the dictionary. ✅
  • Use the blank tile by selecting a letter from the dialog when it appears. ✅
  • Drag tiles from your rack to the board to form words. ✅
  • Click "Submit Word" to validate your word and score points. ✅
  • If you're stuck, click "Deal Tiles" to shuffle your rack. ❌
